The Problem

Pakistan's Retail Is a Data Black Hole

Every thermal paper slip printed is a lost data point. Billions of rupees of invisible economic activity that no one can track.

🌳

Paper Waste and Cost

PKR 500 to 2,000 per month per store on thermal paper alone. Around 360,000 trees cut annually in Pakistan just to print receipts customers lose within minutes.

❌

No Transaction Traceability

Lost receipts create disputes, no refund history, and no audit trail for consumers or retailers. A structural failure with cascading consequences for everyone.

📊

Zero Loyalty Infrastructure

Brands cannot re-engage customers after a sale. Customer loyalty programs are virtually non-existent at the merchant level across Pakistan's retail sector.

🔀

Fragmented POS Ecosystem

Data sits siloed across dozens of incompatible systems with no unified intelligence layer connecting merchants to their customer behaviour and trends.

🏛️

FBR Compliance Gaps

FBR cannot audit real-time transaction data at scale. Every unrecorded receipt represents a tax gap and massive national revenue leakage.

💸

No Affordable Analytics

Merchants want customer data but cannot afford enterprise BI tools. DigiSlip fills this gap at a price below what they already spend on thermal paper every month.

How It Works

Four Steps. Under Two Seconds.

DigiSlip sits between your existing POS and the customer as a lightweight middleware. No hardware change. No app required on either side.

1

Customer Pays at Existing POS

Payment is processed on the merchant's existing terminal as usual. There is absolutely no change to the checkout process or staff workflow.

2

QR Code Appears at Counter

DigiSlip generates a transaction-linked QR code instantly on the POS screen or a small display. The customer scans it with their phone camera in one tap.

3

Identity Matched in the Cloud

DigiSlip middleware captures the transaction and matches the customer via their scanned QR or registered phone number. First-time users register in seconds.

4

Receipt Delivered on WhatsApp

A fully itemised digital receipt arrives on WhatsApp in under 2 seconds. Loyalty points are credited automatically and the receipt is permanently saved in the cloud wallet.
